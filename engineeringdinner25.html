The 4th KESS Engineering Dinner: A Night of Connection and Cuisine

September 27, 2025 Kabale University Engineering Students Society (KESS) University Events

They say the best ideas are forged over good food This proven theory was put into practice on 27th September 2025 as various engineering students and faculty administration gathered at Bunyonyi Safaris Resort for the 4th KESS annual Engineering Dinner. The event, a a highlight of the academic calendar was designed with a splendid theme: "Elevate: Where Engineering meets Elegance." The Chief Guest of Honor was the current UIPE Chairperson UIPE Mbarara, Eng. Hangston.

The evening provided a vital opportunity for students from different disciplines and year groups to connect. Conversations flowed as easily as the dinner service, with first-year students finding mentors in seniors, and peers discussing everything from challenging University life to future career aspirations. These informal interactions are crucial for building the support networks that help students thrive. 2 Faculty members were also present, not as instructors, but as colleagues and fellow engineers. These interactions demystify the lecturer-student relationship and often lead to valuable mentorship and research opportunities. The success of this event underscores the importance of a strong engineering community. A wonderful faculty life, filled with events like these, enhances the student experience, provides opportunities for growth, and ultimately contributes to higher student satisfaction and development.

Highlights

As the evening concluded, the sense of camaraderie was palpable The 4th KESS Annual Engineering Dinner was a powerful reminder that our faculty is more than a collection of brilliant individuals-it is a collaborative supportive and vibrant community. We extend our gratitude to everyone who attended and helped organize this wonderful event. We look forward to seeing these connections flourish throughout the coming years.
